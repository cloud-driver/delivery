[選擇餐廳]
   ↓
GET /api/restaurant/
GET /api/restaurant/<id>

[查看菜單]
   ↓
GET /api/menu/<restaurant_id>

[加入購物車]
   ↓
POST /api/cart
PUT /api/cart/<dish_id>
DELETE /api/cart

[結帳 → 建立訂單（含扣庫存）]
   ↓
POST /api/order
→ DB Transaction：扣庫存 + 建立訂單（Atomic）

[查詢訂單]
   ↓
GET /api/order/<order_id>

[付款完成]
   ↓
PUT /api/order/<order_id>/payment
→ 更新 payment.status = "paid"

[餐廳準備中]
   ↓
PUT /api/order/<order_id>/status
→ 更新 status = "preparing"

[餐點完成]
   ↓
PUT /api/order/<order_id>/status
→ 更新 status = "completed"

[取消訂單（可選）]
   ↓
DELETE /api/order/<order_id>
→ 更新 status = "cancelled"
